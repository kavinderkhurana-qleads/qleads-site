Broker questions

The quick answers.

What exactly is a Q-Lead?
A Dubai property buyer who has had a real WhatsApp conversation with AiSHA, had their budget, purpose and area confirmed, been voice-verified on a recorded call, and consented to speak with one specialist broker — you.
Are the leads really exclusive?
Yes. Every lead is delivered to one broker only and is never shared. There's no race to call first and no competing with other agents over the same buyer.
What does a lead cost?
AED 500 per qualified lead, and your very first lead is free — so you can judge the quality before paying anything. Paid leads come from a prepaid wallet you top up in AED 500 steps.
What if a lead isn't genuine?
If a delivered lead doesn't match what was promised, raise it within 24 hours and we replace it. Every lead's voice verification is recorded and held for fair resolution.
