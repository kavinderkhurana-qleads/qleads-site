1. Who can register

qleads.ai is for RERA-registered Dubai real-estate brokers. You confirm your registration details are accurate at sign-up.

2. What a lead is

A "Q-Lead" is an introduction to a property buyer who has been qualified by AiSHA, voice-verified, and has consented to be contacted by one broker. qleads.ai provides the introduction; it is not a party to any transaction between you and the buyer and is not a brokerage.

3. Pricing

  • Leads are AED 500 each.
  • Your first lead is free.
  • Paid leads are drawn from a prepaid wallet topped up in steps of AED 500. There is no mandatory recharge.

4. Exclusivity

Each lead is delivered to one broker only and is never shared with another broker.

5. Disputes & replacement

If a delivered lead does not match what was promised, raise a dispute within 24 hours of delivery. Valid disputes are resolved by a replacement lead. The voice verification for each lead is recorded and held to resolve disputes fairly. Wallet balances (including any reserve) are refundable on account closure.

6. No guarantee of outcome

We qualify and verify every lead, but we do not guarantee that any lead will transact or convert. Conversion depends on your own follow-up and service.

7. Your responsibilities

To the fullest extent permitted by law, our liability is limited to replacing a non-conforming lead or refunding the corresponding wallet amount. Nothing excludes liability that cannot be excluded under UAE law.
