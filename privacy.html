Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains how qleads.ai — operated by ME Universal IoT Technologies FZE LLC, Dubai, UAE ("we", "us") — handles the personal data of brokers who register with us.

1. What we collect

  • Registration details you provide on WhatsApp — your name, WhatsApp number, RERA/BRN, agency and the areas you cover;
  • Account and billing data — your wallet balance, top-ups and lead history;
  • Standard technical data when you use this website.

2. How we use it

  • To verify you as a RERA-registered broker and create your account;
  • To match and deliver qualified, exclusive buyer leads in your areas;
  • To manage your wallet, billing and any lead disputes;
  • To operate and improve the service.

3. Buyer data shared with you

When you receive a lead, we share that buyer's contact details and qualification context solely so you can follow up on the introduction. You must handle that data lawfully, use it only for that purpose, and comply with UAE data-protection law.

4. Sharing

We use trusted service providers (messaging platform, database, payment processor) to run the service. We never sell your personal data.

5. Retention

We keep your data while your account is active and as needed for legal and accounting purposes. You can ask us to close your account and delete your data.

6. Your rights

Under UAE data-protection law (Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021) you may access, correct or delete your personal data and withdraw consent. Contact us using the details below.

7. Contact

Questions about your data: privacy@qleads.ai.

8. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time; the date above reflects the latest version.
